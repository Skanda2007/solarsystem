The Inner and Outer solar system border and the attention grabbers, Our Asteroids and Comets

After the supernovae many small fragments did not form planets or dwarf planets. But these formed Asteroids. These Asteroids settled between the Inner solar system and the Outer solar system. It is the space between Mars and Jupiter . Asteroids vary from size to size, for example the asteroids can be 1 meter or even 10 kilometers.

But some of them did not become Asteroids insted became Comets. Comets are heavenly bodies who revolve around the Sun. Both the asteroids and the comets have similar chemical composition. Both of them are made up of valuable metals. At times these comets enter the earth's atmosphere and starts to burn. This sight is known meteor showers and the comets which are burning is known as shooting star or meteors. There is a hypothesis that the first water(H2O) and oxygen(O) were brought by these comets who did not completely burn up and fell on the earth's surface. Those meteors who do not burn up completely in the atmosphere and fall onto the ground are called meteorites. These meteorites are collected and tested to know the estimated time from the Big Bang.
